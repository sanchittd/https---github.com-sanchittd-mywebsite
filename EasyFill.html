EasyFill is a mobile application that helps ease up the process of filling up gas. Users can choose from getting the type of gas they want, how much of it they want and then having the comfort of knowing that the next time they go in their car, the tank will be filled up. Additonal offerings include having their car serviced at home and having a luxury car wash.


The Problem

Let's be honest, regardless if the sun is shining or the snow is falling, no one likes to stand outside for 10-15 minutes holding on the gas nozzle and watching the numbers on the pump increase...especially if you're in a rush. It's a process most people dislike, from waiting for a pump to free up to filling up the tank and to finally getting back on the road.

The Solution

EasyFill is a mobile application that requires only 3 steps.
1. Open the app
2. Update your current location
3. Hit "Fill it Up" and Sit back & let us do the rest.
As a frequent driver; going to the station and filling up your tank sucks, so why not let it come to you? Within just a few taps have someone from the EasyFill team come and fill up your tank.

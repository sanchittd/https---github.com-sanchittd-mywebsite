"Get closer than ever to your customers. So close that you tell them what they need well before they realize it themselves."

Steve Jobs
When speaking to a client about a project, I strongly believe that understanding who they are first is far more important than what they might want. This gives me the opportunity to connect with them on a
more personal level thus allowing me to understand what is it that they truly want and how I can work with them to achieve it step by step.

"Design can be art. Design can be aesthetics. Design is so simple, that's why it is so complicated."

Paul Boag
When creating the layout of any application on a platform, the first impression should grasp the attention of the user so that they want to continue using it.
As a user interface designer, I truly enjoy creating beautiful aesthetic designs for both Mobile & Web platforms.

"Users are not always logical, at least not on the surface. To be a great designer you need to look a little deeper into how people think and act."

Paul Boag
The user experience is a very important role when a consumer is using the application, if it isn't easy to use, they wont be inclined to use it.
I like to pride myself on trying to make the user eperience feel like there's a human on the other end not a computer.
During the developing stage, depending on the project, I like to follow these 3 basic stages that allows my work process to be easier. After researching and understanding more of what the project is about, I start off with creating a flowchart. This allows me to see how the application is going to work from start to finish.
Once a flowchart is completed, I then create a prototype sketch to get visual understanding of it. This gives me the options to tweak in it ways I feel suitable to make the next stage easier.
From there, I create low and high fidelity mockups that gives anyone looking at it a much detailed visual understanding of what the application is and how it works.

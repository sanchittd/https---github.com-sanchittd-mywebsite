BreezyBrand

Home / Breeze

BreezyBrand is a fashion application concept that allows those that are passionate about fashion trends to be on top of the game. The app allows users to stay up to date with the latest fashion news of their favorite brands/ idols and giving them the freedom of selecting what they want with a guarantee that what they order is always from a verified source and not a replica.

Process


Using balsamiq I created a sample mockup overview of what I would expect the application to look and do. This allowed me to get a general idea of what I am aiming for, allowing me to create a higher fidelity mockup using sketch.

New Arrivals


Users can see the new arrivals of their favorite brands that they follow or the most popular brands that are the fan favorties.

Review


After selecting an item from your breezy category, you can then review them to see if they fit your style and if it does, add it to your cart!

Cart


Whenever you feel like purchasing the breezy items, make your way to the cart and finalize the purchases. It's that simple!

This is a concept idea I came up with after I started having difficulties finding a place to eat nearby that was actually worth the money. Nearby Eats is an application that shows what eateries are nearby that are rated by your peers. This is an ideal application for those that are new to the area or want to try something they haven't without risking their money.

Overview


After using sketch to create a mockup prototype of 'Nearby Eats', I used the software Principle to create and test the application as if it was actually developed. (Click to make it fullscreen)
